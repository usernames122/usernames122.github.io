Volcanic Island

Welcome to the first Roblox Creator Challenge, Volcanic Island! A dinosaur is stranded on a volcano and needs your help to get her to safety.

Earn badges and prizes as we teach you everything you need to know to build bridges, create new landscape, and use code to create new challenges for your players to face on their path to safety. After all, we can’t make things too easy for the players can we?

Escape the Volcano

Download Roblox Studio and use planks to cross dangerous lava

Traps & Health Parts

Customize code and create new challenges for your players

Terrain and Fire Balls

Create new terrain and use scripts to rain fireballs from the sky
